Our Promise

Welcome to the ROLO Bakery website, the home of Traditional Rye Bread
and Classic Cakes Bakery on the East Coast of England. We take pride in crafting the finest bread,
an exquisite selection of confectionery, and cakes for all your special occasions.
Everything is freshly baked and prepared on-site, using only the highest quality ingredients.
Our range of products is meticulously crafted
by hand, following timeless recipes, and baked in original brick-lined ovens,
imparting a unique taste, flavor, and crust to our bread. Freshness and quality are the hallmarks
of our products, and we are proud of the exceptional standards we maintain.
Some of our breads are crafted with 100% rye flour, while others incorporate varying percentages.
The influence of rye flour on dough handling and the resulting taste and texture of our bread varies
according to the proportion of rye used. This diversity allows our customers to
discover and savor their own favorite taste. Thank you for choosing Harvest Bakery for your culinary delights.
